Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 27, 2026

Smart Form Filler is a Chrome extension that helps users fill web forms with fictional data. This policy explains what data is handled and how.

Data stored on your device

Saved profiles (form field values) are stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage. This data never leaves your device unless you use the AI Fill feature.

Protected profiles are encrypted on your device using a PIN you set. The PIN is never stored anywhere — not on your device, not on our servers.

Data sent to external services

When you use AI Fill, the following information is sent to our server for processing:

  • Form field metadata — field names, types, and placeholders (e.g. name="email", type="text")
  • No form field values are ever sent
  • No personal information is transmitted

This metadata is forwarded to Groq's API to generate realistic fictional data. Cloudflare temporarily logs server activity for up to 7 days for operational purposes, after which logs are deleted automatically.

Data we do not collect

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not track browsing activity
  • We do not use analytics or advertising
  • We do not have user accounts

Third party services

  • Cloudflare — hosts our server
  • Groq — processes AI requests
